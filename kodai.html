OOTY-KODAI

DAY1

Arrival at Coimbatore. Pick up from Railway station / Airport & proceed to Ooty. After arrival check in at Hotel. Evening free at your disposal. Overnight stay at Ooty.

DAY2

Full day sight seeing of Ooty & Coonoor. Overnight stay at Ooty.

DAY3

Morning departure to Kodaikanal, En route see the Silver Cascade waterfall. After arrival at Kodaikanal check into hotel. Overnight stay at Kodaikanal.

DAY4

Half day sight seeing of Kodaikanal. Evening enjoy boating at Kodai Lake. Overnight stay at Kodaikanal.

DAY5

Transfer to Madurai / Coimbatore Railway station / Airport.
