HYDERABAD

DAY1

After arrival at Hyderabad, pick up from Railway station / Airport & transfer to Hotel. Half day sight seeing of Hyderabad. Overnight stay at Hyderabad.

DAY2

Morning departure to Ramoji Film city. Ind yourself in the sets of Bollywood Cinema. Evening visit snow world & shopping mall in Hyderabad city. Overnight stay at Hyderabad.

DAY3

Morning departure to Nagarjunsagar. Visit Nagarjunsagar Dam. Afternoon proceed to Srishailyam. Overnight stay at Srishailyam, Devasthan rooms.

DAY4

Visit Bramharambha Malikarjun Swamy Temple. Transfer to Hyderabad Railway Station / Airport.
