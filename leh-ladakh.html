LEH-LADAKH

DAY1

Pune to Delhi-Srinagar by air and stay in the House boat near Nagin Lake/ Hotel. In the evening Shikara ride in Nagin Lake ( If time & weather permits)

DAY2

Drive from Srinagar and proceed to Kargil via Zoji la Pass & Drass.Visit War memorial in Drass

DAY3

Drive from Kargil to Leh via Mulbek, and Lamayuru monestry Stay at Leh.

DAY4

Local sight seeing in Leh like Shanti stupa, Thiksey Monestry & Shey Palace. Hall of Fame Musium, Magnetic hill, Sindhu Sangam and Gurudwara PatherSahib.

DAY5

Drive to Pengong Lake via 3rd World' highest motorable road "Changla Pass "and stay Leh.

DAY6

Drive to Nubra valley via Diskit over Khardungla Pass the World' highest motorable road .In the evening Camel Safari in Hunder Sand dunes.

DAY7

Drive to Kargil. Stay at Kargil

Kargil to Srinagar
